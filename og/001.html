Heavy menstrual bleeding (G)

Posted: 29/01/2023 | Last updated: 29/01/2023

As the name says… this is when the period is too heavy! その名の通り...月経が重すぎる場合です!

But, you might ask… how heavy is it?

Well, it is when the woman bleeds 80 mL or MORE!!!! So much blood :( まあ、80mL以上の出血があった場合です。そんなに血が出るんだ :(

Causes

There are many causes for this. その原因はさまざまです。

The most common cause for heavy bleeding is… FIBROIDS!!!! 大量出血の一番の原因は... FIBROIDS!!!!

Abnormal uterine bleeding (G)

Posted: 29/01/2023 | Last updated: 29/01/2023

This is when the woman has problems with the menstrual cycle. これは、女性の月経周期に問題がある場合です。

So, it include things like:

  • changes in frequency of the bleeding 出血の頻度の変化
  • duration of the bleed 出血の持続時間
  • amount of bleeding 出血量

There is a mnemonic to help you remember the differetial diagnoses for abnormal uterine bleeding: PALM-COEIN! COEIN looks like coin… 笑。

The PALM part is a mnemonic for the structural causes of abnormal uterine bleeding PALMの部分は、子宮異常出血の構造的な原因を表すニーモニックです。:

  • Polyps
  • Adenomyosis (this is when there is cells from the endometrium start growing in the myometrium [the smooth muscle layer of the uterus]) (子宮内膜の細胞が子宮筋層（子宮の平滑筋層）で増殖し始めることです。）
  • Leiomyomas (this is another name for fibroids)
  • Malignancy

The COEIN part is a mnemonic for the NON-structural causes of abnormal uterine bleeding 子宮異常出血の非構造的な原因を表すニーモニックのCOEINの部分です。:

  • Coagulopathy (bleeding issues)
  • Ovulatory issues
  • Endometrial (problems with the endometrium of the uterus)
  • Iatrogenic (this is when the doctors cause the damage) 医原病

Most common type of abnormal uterine bleeding is Heavy Menstrual Bleeding! 子宮の異常出血で最も多いのは「重い月経血」! This is also called menorrhagia! これは「menorrhagia」とも呼ばれます。
