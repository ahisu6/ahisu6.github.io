Background

The word モールス looks like スモール… ひひひ。

I found this cool article online which has listed all of the Hiragana and Katakana Morse codes. In Japanese, this is called "Wabun code"! ひらがなとカタカナのモールス符号をすべて列挙したクールな記事をネットで見つけた。日本語では「和文モールス符号」と呼ばれる！

Then, I used this Wikipedia page to convert it (with an added hair space to the beginning of the Japanaese morse code to allow for differentiation between the two)!
