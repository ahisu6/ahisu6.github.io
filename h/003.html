Urology

Posted: 2024-02-13 | Updated: 2024-02-13

In urology, there are a couple of possible symptoms that patients might have:

  • Haematuria
  • Dysuria
  • Polyuria
  • Urethral discharge
  • Urgency

Here is a picture to help you remember the important questions:

  • Once upon a time, Socrates was travelling through a stream, using a boat. 昔々、ソクラテスは小舟で小川を旅していた。
    • SOCRATES, urinary stream (小川), blood at which point along stream (小舟)
  • He had a big bag of coins that were running through an hourglass, this helped Socrates keep track of time. 彼は砂時計の中を走っていたコインの大きな袋を持っていた、これはソクラテスが時間を追跡するのに役立ちました。
    • amount of blood (コインの大きな袋), time taken to empty bladder (砂時計の中)
  • Socrates came to a dam that was blocked by clots, many clots. The dam looked like it was in great pain ソクラテスは、血の塊（多くの血の塊）で塞がれたダムにやってきた。ダムは大きな痛みに苦しんでいるように見えた。
    • clot (血の塊), frequency (多くの血の塊), pain (痛み)
  • After a short while, the dam started to bruise until it broke open. That dam was not in a great shape. しばらくすると、ダムは決壊するまで傷つき始めた。そのダムはあまりいい状態ではなかった。
    • bruise (傷つき), trauma (決壊する), constitutional (ダムはあまりいい状態ではなかった)

Now, we need to ask questions about these things:

  1. Good morning, my name is Dr. XYZ. Can I get your name and date of birth please? おはようございます、私はドクターXYZと申します。あなたのお名前と生年月日をお聞かせください。
  2. Thank you for coming today. 本日はご来場いただきありがとうございました。
  3. How can I help you?
  4. Give the patient some time to explain. 患者さんに説明する時間を与えてください。
  5. Right, so you have been experiencing some issues with your waterworks (Waterworks means urinary). Would it be okay if I ask you some more questions? Everything you say will be confidential. そうですか、水道（水道は泌尿器の意味）に問題があるのですね。もう少し質問してもいいですか？おっしゃることはすべて秘密厳守です。
  6. Can you describe the issue for me? この問題について説明してもらえますか？
  7. Give the patient some time to explain. 患者さんに説明する時間を与えてください。
  8. Do DOP: How long have you had this for? When did it start? How did it start? Is it getting worse? DOP：いつからですか？いつからですか？どのように始まりましたか？悪化していますか？
  9. Now, I would like to ask you some specific questions if that is okay. では、いくつか具体的な質問をしたいのですが、よろしいですか？
  10. Do you have any difficulty with passing urine? 尿が出にくいことはありますか？
  11. Do you have any blood in urine? If the patient says "yes" then ask these questions 尿に血液は混じっていますか？もし患者が「はい」と答えたら、次のような質問をします:
    1. I am sorry to hear that. It must be quite distressing. (You can do ICE here!). お気の毒です。とてもつらいことでしょう。(ICEはここでできます！）。
    2. What colour is it? 色は？
    3. Does the blood come at the start, middle, or the end of the stream? 血は流れの最初、真ん中、それとも最後？
    4. Are there any clots? 血栓はありますか？
  12. How often are you going to the toilet during the day? 日中、どのくらいの頻度でトイレに行きますか？
  13. How often are you going to the toilet during the night? 夜間のトイレの回数は？
  14. Does it take a long time to empty your bladder? 膀胱を空にするのに時間がかかりますか？
  15. Is there any pain? If the patient says "yes" then ask these questions 痛みはありますか？もし患者が「はい」と答えたら、次のような質問をします:
    1. Do SOCRATES
  16. Have you noticed any bruising anywhere? どこかにあざはありましたか？
  17. Have you had any physical trauma? 身体的なトラウマはありますか？
  18. Have you been sick recently? 最近体調を崩されましたか？
  19. Have you had fever? 熱がありましたか？
  20. Have you had night sweats? 寝汗をかいたことはありますか？
  21. Have you noticed any weight changes? 体重に変化はありましたか？
  22. Are you eating and drinking well? よく食べ、よく飲んでいるか？
  23. Are you sleeping well? よく眠れていますか？
  24. Do a quick summary. 簡単なまとめをしてください。
  25. Do ICE.

Tired all the time

Posted: 2023-10-08 | Updated: 2023-10-08

Many things can cause tiredness and fatigue:

  • Cancer
  • Depression
  • Thyroid issues
  • Diabetes
  • Infection
  • Sleep issues
  • And many more!

Anyway, here is what you should ask:

  1. Good morning, my name is Dr. XYZ. Can I get your name and date of birth please? おはようございます、私はドクターXYZと申します。あなたのお名前と生年月日をお聞かせください。
  2. Thank you for coming today. 本日はご来場いただきありがとうございました。
  3. How can I help you?
  4. Right, so you have been experiencing some tiredness. Can you tell me a little bit more about that?
  5. Give the patient some time to explain. 患者さんに説明する時間を与えてください。
  6. I will just do a quick summary of what you have told me so far.
  7. Do a quick summary. 簡単なまとめをしてください。
  8. So, how long have you been feeling tired for?
  9. When did it start?
  10. Has it become better or worse?
  11. Is there a particular time of day where you feel most tired?
  12. Does anything make it worse?
  13. Do you have any ideas, concerns, and expectations?
  14. Is it okay if I ask you few more questions?
  15. How is your appetite?
  16. How is your mood?
  17. How is your sleep?
  18. Have you had any recent infections?
  19. Have you noticed weight changes?
  20. Do you feel cold when others think it's warm? (you are checking for hypothyroidism).
  21. How are your waterworks? (Waterworks means urinary).
  22. How are your bowel habits?
  23. Any bleeding from anywhere?
  24. Any pain?
  25. Do you feel weak?
  26. How is your concentration?
  27. Do a quick summary. 簡単なまとめをしてください。
  28. Do ICE.
  29. Then, do the rest of the history (past medical, past surgical, etc.)
